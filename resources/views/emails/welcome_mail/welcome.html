If you are transferring from another retailer

Once transferred your previous energy company will send you a final bill and from then on your bill will come from {{electricity_provider_name}}.

Your account will be transferred on or before your next scheduled meter read. Depending on where you are in the billing cycle this could take up to 4 months. Please note: If you have a smart meter your new provider does not need to wait for your next scheduled meter read to transfer your electricity account so this may happen much faster.

If you are moving property:

It is your responsibility to tell your current supplier(s) that you are moving out of your current property and that you would like the electricity to be disconnected.

The new connection may be done any time up until midnight on the day of transfer. As such, if you need the connection to be done early in the day, we recommend you speak to {{electricity_provider_name}} about changing it to the day before
